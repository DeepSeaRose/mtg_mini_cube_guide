<details style="width:100%;float:none;">
<summary>Black/Green Graveyard</summary>
<p>
Dig through bones and refuse to find the power in the discarded. 
</P>
<p>
Fill your graveyard with creature and artefact cards by utilising mill and discard effects, before unleashing a big threat that benefits from the corpses! (Just don't forget to add some return from the graveyard effects to keep you going into the late game)
</p>

<summary>Black/Red Sacrifices</summary>

<p>
Take on the persona of a maniacal villain, thoughtlessly sacrificing your own minions for personal gain! 
</p>
<p>
Cast small creatures in your first few turns (preferably creatures that can come back from the dead, or provide additional benefits when they die) to eke out some early damage out, then sacrifice those creatures for value once they have outlived their usefulness!
</p>

<p>
Decieve your enenemies, sneaking past defences and carefully countering their advances with dark magics. 
</p>
<p>
Use small hard to block creatures to slowly chip away at your opponents health and draw cards, while holding up mana to counter and remove any threats they might dare to point your way.

<summary>Blue/Red Spellslinger</summary>
<p>
The play-style of a true(ly mad) wizard! Sling spells, play fast, and try to point the big monsters you've attracted at your opponent! 
</p>
<p>
Play lots of cheap direct damage and draw spells early on to make your opponent vulnerable and maintain card advantage, then finish with a huge creature that cares about the amount of instants/sorceries you've already cast!
</p>

<summary>Green/Blue Growth</summary>

<p>
For you mass is just a number to be altered. Imbued with the powers of unnatural growth even your tiniest threats could become gigantic monsters at a moments notice. 
</p>
<p>
Utilise blue's assortment of small evasive creatures to avoid your opponents blockers, than use instant speed combat tricks to grow their power and toughness before they can react! 
</p>
